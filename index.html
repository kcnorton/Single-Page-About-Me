Software Engineering

I'm pursuing my Master's in Computer Science from Georgia Tech. My specialization is in Computing Systems. Throughout my courses, I developed a database application from scratch, implemented test driven development for small Android apps, written a research paper on game-based learning and gender-inclusive game design, and more. I've also built an e-commerce web app during a self-paced full stack course. In addition, I completed a CS Bridge Program through NYU in preparation for my Master's.

Rock Climbing

Growing up, I learned how to rock climb at a summer camp in North Carolina. About 9 years ago, I found a local climbing gym, signed up for a membership, and been climbing ever since. From local competitions to outside climbing destinations to indoor route setting, the climbing community has become an integral part of my life.

Distance Running

I've always enjoyed running as an activity, but in 2015 my two sisters and I signed up for the Chicago Marathon together. Before that, I had never run more than 8 miles at a time. Now distance running has become one of my favorite hobbies. I've run a total of 5 marathons with more on the docket.
